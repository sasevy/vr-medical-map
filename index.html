About this Map

This map visualizes the adoption of Virtual Reality (VR) in medical training programs worldwide as of 2024. The data highlights the significant digital divide in access to this transformative technology.

Notable initiatives include Kenya's partnership with Meta to bring VR labs to rural clinics, and similar programs emerging in Southeast Asia.

Data sources: Compiled from various academic studies, industry reports, and healthcare technology surveys. The adoption rate represents the percentage of medical institutions that have integrated VR into their training curricula.
